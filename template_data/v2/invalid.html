How edX Validates Student Certificates

Certificates issued by edX are signed by a gpg key so that they can be validated independently by anyone with the edX public key. For independent verification, edX uses what is called a "detached signature."

About edX Certificates

EdX acknowledges achievements through certificates, which are awarded for various activities edX students complete under the edX Honor Code. Some certificates require completing additional steps, such as verifying one's identity.
